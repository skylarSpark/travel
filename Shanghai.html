Where to go:

The Bond 外滩 Waitan

Iconic riverside promenade with colonial-era buildings facing the Pudong Lujiazui skyline. Best visited at sunset or after dark when the lights come on.

Yu Garden 豫园 Yuyuan

A classical Ming-dynasty garden in the heart of the Old Shanghai City. Surrounding bazaar is free to explore, just enjoy the vibe.

Anfu Road 安福路

Located at the Former French Concession, it's filled with tree-lined streets and Tudor-style mansions. This area is perfect for a leisurely afternoon.

Things to do:

Bike share along Suzhou Creek

Rent a shared bike and ride the scenic waterway through historic neighbourhoods.

Night view of Pudong from the Bund

No ticket needed — just stand on the riverside walkway and look east after dark.

Ride MagLav end-to-end

Travel from Pudong Airport to the center Pudong on a 400km/h highspeed magnetic powered train. It is a great way to see the city.
